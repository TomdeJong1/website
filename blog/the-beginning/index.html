The Beginning

29 Oct, 2022 | 1 minute read
Share this: Twitter Pinterest

I will start as a freelance workspace consultant from the start of January 2023. My focus lies on the Citrix perspective and Microsoft Azure platform.

I’ve started my IT journey at the Royal Signal Battalion in the Royal Netherlands Army as a network admin. From there I joined a production company where I had my first encounter with Citrix and techniques as NVIDIA vGPU and CAD/CAM solutions like Solidworks and Autodesk applications. Part time I’m also writing and running tests for G0-EUC. At G0-EUC our mission is to help others creating data on how environments perform under different circumstances and what the impact of changes are the environments.

As a non-commissioned officer in the army, you were besides a leader also an instructor. From there on I’ve enrolled into the Microsoft Certified Trainer and Citrix Certified Instructor role. At this moment I’m allowed to teach almost all Citrix courses and working on the Microsoft part.

Since a year or two I’ve been enrolling more into the DevOps way of working, especially with Ansible and Hashicorp products Terraform and Packer. With this way of working you will have a robust environment, self describing and also quickly the restore in a disaster recovery.
